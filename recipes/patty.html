The Krabby Patty

The Krabby Patty is the Krusty Krab's signature food item

It is a meatless hamburger of sorts made from seaweed-sea buns, undersea vegetables, condiments, and a patty. It can also be ordered with cheese..

Ingredients

  • Bottom bun
  • Patty of choice
  • Lettuce
  • cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Pickles
  • The "secret sauce"
  • Love
  • Top bun

Steps

  1. Shine that bun
  2. Grill that patty
  3. Assemble your patty with ingredients (extra love)
